What Are Raw Indian Hair Bundles?

Raw Indian hair refers to 100% unprocessed human hair collected from a single donor. Unlike many commercial hair extensions, raw hair is not chemically treated, steam processed, or silicone coated.

The hair typically comes from temple donations in South India, where individuals voluntarily donate their hair as part of religious tradition.

Key characteristics of authentic raw Indian hair:
	•	Cuticle aligned
	•	Single donor bundles
	•	Natural texture (not steam processed)
	•	Can be colored or bleached
	•	Long lifespan when properly maintained

Because the hair is unprocessed, each bundle may have slightly different wave patterns. This is a sign of authenticity rather than a defect.

Why Raw Indian Hair Is Considered the Gold Standard

Professional stylists often prefer raw Indian hair because of its durability and natural appearance.

Benefits include:
	•	Holds curls and waves well
	•	Blends easily with natural hair
	•	Can be colored safely
	•	Minimal tangling and shedding
	•	Typically lasts several years with proper care

These qualities make raw Indian hair popular among both salon professionals and retail customers in the USA.
